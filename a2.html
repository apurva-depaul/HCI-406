How to Make a Cup of Tea

Step 1: Boil Water

Fill a kettle with fresh water and bring it to a boil.

Step 2: Place Tea Bag in Cup

Put your favorite tea bag into a clean cup or mug.

Step 3: Pour Hot Water

Carefully pour the hot water into the cup over the tea bag.

Step 4: Steep the Tea

Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on how strong you like it.

Step 5: Add Sugar or Milk

Add sugar, milk, or lemon to taste. Stir and enjoy your tea!
